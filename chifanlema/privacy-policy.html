Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Chifanlema ("we", "our", or "the app") is a Mandarin Chinese vocabulary learning app. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the following data when you use Chifanlema:

  • Email address — used to create and manage your account.
  • User ID — a unique identifier assigned to your account by our authentication system.
  • Learning activity — which vocabulary words you have seen, your selected HSK level, and daily word goal. This is used to personalize your word feed and avoid showing repeated words.
  • Search queries — when you use the in-app dictionary search, your query is sent to our server to return results. Search queries are processed in real time and are not stored.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Account management — to let you sign in, manage your profile, and sync progress across devices.
  • App functionality — to deliver personalized vocabulary based on your level and learning history.
  • Search — to process dictionary search requests in real time.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any third party. Your data stays on our servers (hosted on Supabase, with infrastructure on AWS in the Asia Pacific region).

When you sign in with Google, Facebook, or Apple, those providers may collect data according to their own privacy policies. We only receive your email address and basic profile information from them.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption. Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Supabase with row-level security policies. Passwords are never stored in plain text — authentication is handled by Supabase Auth using industry-standard hashing.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data (profile, learning history, seen words) is permanently deleted within 48 hours.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time via the Settings screen in the app or through our account deletion page.
  • Request your data by contacting us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

Chifanlema is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
