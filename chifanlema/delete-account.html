Delete Your Account

To delete your Chifanlema account and all associated data (learning progress, seen words, profile), follow these steps:

  1. Open the Chifanlema app on your device
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon in the top right)
  3. Scroll down to Data & Privacy
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion

Your account and all data will be permanently deleted. This action cannot be undone.

If you no longer have access to the app, contact us at hungcq.chifanlema@gmail.com with the email address associated with your account, and we will process the deletion within 48 hours.
