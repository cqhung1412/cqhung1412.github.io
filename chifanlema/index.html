Core Experience

Word Feed

Swipe through vocabulary in a full-screen vertical feed. "Daily Words" gives you a personalized set based on your HSK level, while "For You" adapts to your learning history.

AI Chat Search

Ask anything in English, Vietnamese, or Chinese. Get streaming AI responses with word breakdowns, example sentences, and contextual follow-up suggestions.

Stroke Order

Interactive character writing with HanziWriter. See each stroke animated step-by-step. Tap individual characters in multi-character words for detailed breakdowns.

Native Pronunciation

Auto-play audio as you scroll. AI-generated TTS for word pronunciation plus real native speaker recordings from Tatoeba for example sentences.

Lock Screen Widgets

Learn passively throughout the day. iOS WidgetKit and Android Glance widgets show your daily words right on your lock screen.

Collections & History

Browse words by HSK level or 77 topic categories. Track your viewing history, bookmark favorites, and organize words into personal collections.

Shareable Word Cards

Generate beautiful 4:5 word cards with pinyin, meaning, and example sentences. Save to your photo library or share directly to social media.

Voice Search

Speak in Chinese, English, or Vietnamese to search. Speech recognition built into the floating search bar for hands-free word lookup.

Bilingual Interface

Full English and Vietnamese UI. Word definitions, example sentences, and AI responses available in both languages.

About Chifanlema

Chifanlema (吃饭了吗 — "Have you eaten?") is a micro-learning app that makes Mandarin Chinese vocabulary stick through daily repetition and an engaging, scroll-friendly format. Over 25,000 words covering HSK levels 1–6 and 77 topic categories, with bilingual definitions in English and Vietnamese.

The app combines AI-powered search, animated stroke order, real native speaker audio from Tatoeba, and shareable word cards — all wrapped in a warm minimalist design that works beautifully in both light and dark mode.

Built by an indie developer in Vietnam. Free to use with an optional premium subscription for an ad-free experience and unlimited AI searches.
