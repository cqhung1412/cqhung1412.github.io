About Chifanlema

Chifanlema (吃饭了吗 — "Have you eaten?") is a micro-learning app that teaches Mandarin Chinese vocabulary through lock screen widgets and a TikTok-style word feed. Pick your HSK level, set a daily goal, and learn new words every day.

The app features AI-powered search, stroke order animations, example sentences with audio from Tatoeba, and shareable word cards for social media. Available in English and Vietnamese.

Built by an indie developer in Vietnam. Free to use with optional premium subscription for an ad-free experience and unlimited AI searches.
